Periotris.js is your best companion for learning and memorizing the Periodic Table of Elements.

Combining the classic game experience of Tetris with the periodic table, Periotris.js is a successful attempt of breathing vitality into the boredom of chemistry learning.

The rumor that periodic table is constructed out of a game of Tetris has been brought to reality. By playing Periotris.js you can experience the thrill of fitting elements into right slots like a real chemist. Despite its simplicity, Periotris.js is a challenging game that puts the player's knowledge of descriptive chemistry to the test. Try finish the game in the shortest time possible and experience the excitement of completing another brick in the wall of chemistry.
