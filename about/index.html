Periotris.js is your best companion for learning and memorizing the Periodic Table of Elements.

Combining the classic game experience of Tetris with the periodic table, Periotris.js is a successful attempt of breathing vitality into the boredom of chemistry learning.

The rumor that periodic table is constructed out of a game of Tetris has been brought to reality. By playing Periotris.js you can challenge yourself on reaction speed while refreshing your periodic table knowledge. You need to be quite familiar with heavy elements in later periods to finish the game! Try to finish the game in the shortest time possible and experience the excitement of fitting another brick into the wall of chemistry.

Built with ❤ by CSharperMantle. Special thanks to Mr. Longdi Xu, my high school chemistry teacher, for sparking the idea that finally went so far as this.
